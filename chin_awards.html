Post office competition stage 1 selection

A team from my programming design and applications course participated in the National Postal Office Artificial Intelligence competition and cleared the first stage of evaluations. Out of the 401 participating teams from all over Taiwan, only 60 teams passed this first stage. Our team has worked hard toward practical data-driven ideation and design. An interesting fact is the 4 students in our team (2 students are Industrial Engineering majors, 1 student is from economics, and 1 student from the mathematics department). We are now working towards round 2.
